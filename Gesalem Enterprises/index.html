About Us

Established in 2011, Gesalem Enterprises is a multifaceted business enterprise owned and operated by Engr. Mark U. Gesalem.

The company has diversified operations spanning across trading, manufacturing, and service sectors. With its strong entrepreneurial spirit and commitment to excellence, Gesalem Enterprises has carved a niche for itself in the industry, catering to a wide range of clients with diverse requirements.



Mission

At "Gesalem Enterprises", is to be the premier global trading company, enabling the seamless flow of merchandise across diverse industries and markets. We aim to be the partner of choice for our clients by offering comprehensive, end-to-end trading solutions tailored to fulfil their unique requirements.
